PLAY TABLETURF NOW

The best matchmaking service for Tableturf Battle!

It has never been easier to play PvP Tableturf Battle before! Just enter your username to join the queue, and you will get automatically matched with another player ready for battle!

Simple and fun casual matchmaking

If you want a more relaxed environment, jump into the casual queue and play however many games you want with your opponent! There’s no restrictions on stages or a timer. Just play and have fun!

Want to test your skills?

Prove yourself by climbing ranks and leaderboards in ranked matchmaking! Play a set against your opponent with the standard competitive ruleset, and receive your rank after having played 3 placement matches. Ranked is restricted to users logged in with Discord.

TableturfQ offers:

An easy to use matchmaking system

Get matched with other players with the click of a button!

A beginner friendly casual mode

Battle with your opponent for as long as you want, and leave at any time. No risks, no restrictions, no pressure.

A competitive queue for glory

Try your hardest in these thrilling, competitive matches! Play a best of 5 set against your opponent, increasing or decreasing your rating based on your performance.

A chat for easy communication

Make sure to be nice and respectful. Read our rules (link to rules) for more information. Join our discord to report inappropriate users.

A leaderboard

Climb to the top of the leaderboard, showing off your amazing skills!

A Discord server

Discuss Tableturf Battle, join events, and share decks in the official Tableturf Battle Discord server!
